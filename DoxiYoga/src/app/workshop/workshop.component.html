Intro to Backband Workshop

What would you find here:

In this intense workshop, we are working in a small group. We'll be going back to basics on how to approach a safe curve for the spine. You'll have enough space and time to really get to grips with what you need to know when you are ready for a wheel pose. We warm up the body, which is followed by standing and sitting poses, then finally going through some Asanas to build strength to the core for a peak pose, after that cooling down our body with a closing sequence. We hold asanas for 5 deep breaths and notice them.

Areas covered include:

Wrist, shoulder, core strength and mobility Integrate your muscles to keep you safe and strong in backbends Practices by the wall Safe entry and exit
